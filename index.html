Buying Guide to the Perfect Cabin Bag with Front Laptop Pocket

Here is a simple, straightforward, buying guide to help you pick the perfect bag to meet your needs

What is a Cabin Bag with a Front Laptop Pocket?

A cabin bag that provides a big enough front pocket to safely store a laptop computer, a tablet or both.

Why would you need a bag like this?

By having the laptop pouch at the front of the bag it makes it very accessible while the bag is stored or stowed to get your device.

Important Features for a Cabin Bag with Front Laptop Pocket

Here is an explanation of all the important features to consider when buying a Cabin Bag with a Laptop Pocket. You can filter the results above on each of these features to ensure you find just the right case for you.

  • USB Charger: This means the cabin bag includes the ability to plug in a usb device to the bag to enable it to to charge. A power pack or similar device will need to be placed inside the bag for this to work.
  • Spinner: This means the cabin bag includes four wheels that can all move indepenndently of each other and allow you to spin or move the case in any direction.
  • Weight: How heavy is your bag before you put any luggage in.
  • Wheels: Does the bag have wheels to help reduce the need to carry a heavy bag around an airport, train station, hotel or ferry.
  • Expandable: Does the bag provide an option to expand the sides to be able to fit in more luggage for either leg of your trip.
  • TSA Lock: A TSA compatible lock that allows Transport Security Administrators to easily open and inspect your luggage.
  • Ryanair Underseat Compatible: This indicates that the laptop is small enough to fit under the seat on a Ryanair flight.

If a front laptop pocket better than a rear laptop pocket?

This depends on your personal needs and preference. If you need very quick and easy access to your device then a cabin bag with a front laptop pocket is the best option.

If you don't and want to make sure your laptop is as protected as possible in your bag then you may be better off a a cabin bag with a rear or mid bag laptop pocket.

How Much Should I pay for a Cabin Bag With A Front Laptop Pocket?

Prices for this type of cabin bag range from £25 all the way up to £300+ depending on make, material and features.
